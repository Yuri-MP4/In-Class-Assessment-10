SpookySpaghetti: 2011x


Some time after arriving on Earth, an unknown individual encounters X’s disk, releases it online as a GameJolt game to escape X’s influence, but likely fails, as X seems to control the account following the victim's last post. Players subsequently download and play the game, which begins with a distorted Sonic 1 title screen, leading to a save select featuring Tails, Knuckles, and Dr. Eggman, with Knuckles and Eggman locked from selection.

Upon selecting Tails, he finds himself in "HILL" surrounded by dead animals. He encounters Sonic, who has grey shoes, and attempts to avoid him, but the screen border prevents his escape. After being forced to approach Sonic, a black screen appears with the message "HELLO. DO YOU WANT TO PLAY WITH ME?" Tails is revealed to be Alan and enters a game of HIDE AND SEEK. A countdown starts, leading to a sign that shows "TIME OVER" and "READY OR NOT, HERE I COME." The character X begins to chase Alan, knocking him down and breaking his leg. Unable to escape, Alan is killed when X teleports and snaps his spine, taunting the player with, "AWW… TOO SLOW. WANT TO TRY AGAIN?"

Knuckles' level, "YOU CAN'T RUN", features a dark stage illuminated only by thunder. In this setting, Knuckles, revealed as Dave, encounters X, who taunts him with "FOUND YOU". Despite Dave's attempts to retaliate, X dodges effortlessly and ultimately delivers a brutal kick that knocks out Dave's teeth. X then fatally stabs Dave, expressing satisfaction while reflecting on past victims with the remark, "SO MANY SOULS, SO LITTLE TIME. WOULDN'T YOU AGREE?"

The player would then start up Eggman's level, "…". After walking downstairs for about two minutes, X would suddenly appear in front of Eggman, now revealed to be Paul, scaring him. It would then suddenly open his jaw, which would extend itself in length in an unnatural, impossible way. The screen would be filled with static, and then we'd be shown a black screen with nothing but a text saying "GAME OVER". The letters of said text would be tinted with blood.

We'd be shown the torn apart corpses of the three victims, locked within the save files for each character. Then, the screen would be filled with static, and we'd be shown a close-up of X's eyes. The screen would light itself up and show us X's face, rendered through a 16-bit filter. The said filter would eventually vanish, showing us a realistic close-up of his facial features. It would then write out "I AM GOD" on our screen with his fingers, which were tainted with the blood of his previous victims. It then lunges at the screen, breaking it, and finishing the game.
